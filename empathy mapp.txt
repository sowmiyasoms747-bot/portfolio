Planning For New Movie
Touch Point: Trailer, social media (insta, thread), Movie apps (Paytm Movies, Bookmyshow)
User Action: Watches trailer, wait for release
User Emotion: Excited & Hyped 🤩
Pain Point: No clarity on booking time
Opportunities: Upcoming movies trailer & release date


Booking Opens
Touch Point: Booking app, Movie & Seat Selection
User Action: Checking for showtime, theatres & seats
User Emotion: Hopeful but unsure 😟
Pain Point: No seat hold option
Opportunities: AI pre-set movie booking with Preferred Showtime, Theatre & Seat

Payment Confirmation
Touch Point: Payment gateway (G-pay, Paytm, etc.) Confirmation
User Action: Tries to pay fast to confirm booking
User Emotion: Nervous until confirmation 😬
Pain Point: Payment fail, Seat loss
Opportunities: Auto-payment system
Confirmation
Touch Point: Ticket confirmation
User Action: Check ticket details
User Emotion: Happy & relieved 😊
Pain Point: Majority None
Opportunities: Instant confirmation to Email, Whatsapp
