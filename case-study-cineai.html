The Project

What is BookMyShow AI Smart Booking?

This project introduces an AI-powered pre-booking system that allows users to book tickets even before they go live. Users can set their preferences in advance, and the AI automatically books tickets at the right moment.

The Context

Why did I choose this?

I chose this problem because many users miss tickets for their favorite movies due to high demand and a last-minute rush.

The Objective

Goal

My goal is to make booking simple, smart, and stress-free for everyone.

Real Problems | Smart Solutions.

⚡ Problems

  • Users cannot book tickets before they go live.
  • Tickets sell out quickly during high demand.
  • Users can select only one date and time.
  • Booking is stressful with constant refreshing.
  • Payment often fails during peak time.
  • Users are unsure how booking works.

✦ Solutions

  • Enable AI pre-booking to book tickets automatically at release time.
  • Use AI to book instantly and increase success rate.
  • Allow backup date and time selection for better chances.
  • Provide a hands-free booking experience using AI.
  • Enable AutoPay for smooth and instant transactions.
  • Show clear AI status and booking summaries.

Solving real user problems with simple and smart automation.

Impact that matters

90%Reduction in booking time
3×Faster than manual booking
100%Ensures users don’t miss releases
0App crashes in prototype tests
  • All 5 usability test users successfully completed a booking without assistance.
  • The live status animation eliminated anxiety — users rated "trust in system".
  • Preference setup Simplified selection process, minimizing user drop-off.

What I learned

Trust is a UX problem Progressive disclosure beats long forms Status feedback = lower anxiety AI needs guardrails users can feel Dark UIs need higher contrast ratios Fallback flows are as important as the main flow Test with real stress scenarios, not ideal ones
