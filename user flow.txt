Discovery: User navigates to Upcoming Movies and selects a film.
The Trigger: User clicks the AI - Pre-booking button.
Preferences (The Safety Net):
Select at least 2 preferred dates.
Select 2 preferred theaters and showtimes.
Choose the number of seats.
Select Primary & Secondary seat choices (to ensure a successful booking if the first choice is taken).
Verification: A Preview screen shows all selected preferences for review.
Automation: User sets up Autopay to allow the system to purchase the ticket the moment booking opens.
End Goal: Receive a Confirmation.
Secondary Path: Management
Profile Access: Users can go to their Profile → Your Bookings & Purchases → Pre-booking to track the status of their pending automated tickets.
